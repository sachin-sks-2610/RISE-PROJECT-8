Our Story

We're on a mission to make quality services accessible to everyone

ServeEase was founded in 2020 with a simple idea: connecting skilled professionals with the people who need them should be effortless, transparent, and fair for everyone involved. We've grown from a small startup into a trusted platform serving thousands of homes and businesses every day.

Whether you need a plumber at 7am or a photographer for the weekend, our vetted network of service providers has you covered — with upfront pricing, real reviews, and seamless booking in just a few clicks.

Timeline

Our Journey So Far

From a two-person startup to a nationwide platform — here are the moments that defined us.

2020

Founded in a Garage

Two friends frustrated by unreliable contractors decided to build a better way. ServeEase launched in beta with 12 service providers in one city.

2021

Seed Funding & Expansion

Raised $2M in seed funding and expanded to 5 major cities, onboarding over 300 vetted professionals across 10 service categories.

2022

10,000 Bookings Milestone

Crossed 10,000 completed bookings and launched our mobile-optimized platform alongside the provider rating and review system.

2023

Series A & 25 Cities

Closed a $12M Series A round. Expanded to 25 cities, introduced subscription packages, real-time tracking, and same-day booking.

2024

120,000+ Services & Counting

Now operating in 40+ cities with 2,500+ verified providers and over 120,000 completed services — and we're just getting started.
